SAMPLE OF FIRST WEEK

Week 1 :

Day 1 - Back :

Front WIDE pulldowns - 4 sets 15-12 elbows back when pulling down

Reverse Closegrip pulldowns - 4 sets 12-10 keep biceps near ears when pulling

DB rows HEAVY - 4 sets 12-10 Heavy but good form

T-bar rows - 4 sets 12-10 heavy but good form

Seated Rows - 4 sets 12-10 reps elbows back chest out


Day 2 - Chest:

Flat Chest Press - 4 sets of 10-12 reps

Incline Chest press - 4 sets 8-10 reps

Incline Flies DB- 4 sets of 12-15 reps

Cable Flies High/Low/Medium - 4 sets 12-15 reps each


Day 3 - Legs :

Leg Extensions - 2 warmup sets of 10 - 4 sets of 10-20 reps

Hack Squat - 4 sets of 10-12 reps *NO BOUNCING*

Squats - 4 sets 15-15-12-12 reps

Seated Curls - 4 sets 15-15-12-12 increasing weight each set


Day 4 - Shoulders :

Barbell Wide Upright Rows - 4 sets 10-12 reps

DB Presses - 4 sets 10-12 reps

DB Side Raises - 4 sets 20-15-12-10 increase weight each set

Face Pulls - 4 sets 20-15-12-10 increase weight each set


Day 5 - Arms :

Barbell Curl Wide - 4 sets to failure each set

DB curls - 4 sets to failure then drop 1 time to failure

Heavy DB Curls - 4 sets 8-10 reps increase weight each set

Tricep Pushdown - 4 sets 10-12 reps

Rope Pushdown - 4 sets 10-12 reps Good Squeeze on each rep

Skull Crushers - 4 sets 10-12 reps increase weight each setd
